The Story Behind Catherine Luz

My story is one of transformation, much like the clients who walk through LUMERA’s doors. Born in Bordeaux, I grew up in a nurturing yet disciplined household, shaped by my father’s precision and my mother’s warmth...

With just $7,000 in 2013, I moved to New York... Founding LUMERA in 2015 was a leap of faith... By 2018, LUMERA’s billion-dollar valuation was a testament to my grit and foresight...

My journey isn’t just about financial success; it’s about reclaiming my power after loss and building a legacy that reflects my values...
